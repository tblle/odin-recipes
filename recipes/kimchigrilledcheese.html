Kimchi Grilled Cheese

Crispy on the outside, soft in the inside, and a punch of crunchy spicy, sour surpise in the middle. A quick and simple sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 30g unsalted butter
  • 1 slice of muenster cheese
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 50g of kimchi

Steps

  1. Roughly chop kimchi and fry for a few minutes over med-high heat. Place kimchi to the side.
  2. Heat up half of butter in medium heat. Once butter is no longer in its solid state, place next ingredients in this order: 1 bread, cheddar, kimchi, muenster, last bread.
  3. Cook until bottom bread is golden brown. Before flipping sandwich, place rest of butter in pan and then flip. Remove once both bread are golden brown.
